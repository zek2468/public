Rules

  1. Bigotry and discrimination of any kind is prohibited. This includes hate speech, symbols, jokes, skins and anything else that creates an unsafe environment for people of a particular group.

  2. NSFW text, images, builds, skins, etc will not be permitted.

  3. Be respectful of other players. Respect others boundaries, and do not harass, threaten, impersonate, share personal information without consent, or otherwise intentionally harm others.

  4. Do not grief. This includes both destruction or defacing of builds or nearby areas, as well as unwanted block spam.

  5. Do not kill pets or named mobs you do not own.

  6. No soliciting, begging, scamming, advertising unrelated things, or trading of real currency.

  7. Do not create builds that harm the server or other players.

  8. Do not create afk/mob farms (without a spawner) or devices.

  9. Do not create Massive redstone devices or farms that harm the server in any way.

  10. Mods, exploited bugs, or cheats that give you an unfair advantage over others are strictly prohibited. (Only cosmetic mods like maps/waypoints and optifine are allowed)

  11. No spam, either in chat or via things like teleport requests.

  12. Do not attempt to bypass bans, PvP toggle, filters, afk or similar measures created by the staff.

  13. Do not break any IRL laws.

  14. Do not use alternate accounts.

  15. Do not steal from other players outside of rule 23.

  16. Only use ASCII characters in the chat/server.

  17. Notify a staff member if you see a violation of the rules or if someone is making you uncomfortable for any reason.

  18. Do not directly copy real world nations, cultures or religions by name. Influence is accepted, but copying is not.

  19. Do not build outposts, roads or any other structure near or towards a town without permission first.

  20. You may not engage in PvP/War activities before 48hrs of contacting them with the declaration, as well as staff (so that we may set the war status).

  21. Bandits/Pirates and any other organized crime groups are not allowed.

  22. No Nations/Towns that directly copy from another server

  23. If a home/chest is not in a town or locked, players may take from them

  24. Staff make final decision on rules and punishments please do not argue with them.

  25. When in a time of war/armed conflict, respect the Server War Rules and any rules established in your War Ticket.

  26. Wait a reasonable amount of time before trying to recruit new members that join the server. Instead, redirect them to the discord with /discord and tell them to look through #nation-ads or ask around.

  27. Please appeal all punishment here http://ban.dashworld.xyz:3000/

All server rules will continue to evolve over time.
This means we may take action against any player or group that violates the spirit of these rules when we encounter a new threat or harm that is not explicitly covered in the current version.
