War Rules

  1. Both Leaders must submit a #towny-war-tickets (In discord) UNLESS an effective Casus Belli (Below) is applied to one ticket (with higher staff review)

  2. Nations that are set to Neutral cannot be brought into any war

  3. All wars must be announced 48hrs in advance

  4. Cobelligerents/Allies must be declared in the tickets

  5. All leaders involved in a war must also present a case for war to staff BEFORE ticket is approved.

  6. War Goals must be specified clearly in the ticket.

  7. Only players that are in a town BEFORE the ticket was made may participate in the war. (This is to prevent PvP padding)

  8. Rule violations can not be counted as a cause of war and should be resolved by staff.

  9. City-States/ Nations cannot declare, participate, or be a target of war within 2 weeks of founding.

  10. Offline wars are not allowed. A majority (over 60%) of a town/nation/group on both sides must be online for the war to start.

  11. If a war needs to be postponed/cancelled, you must inform all involved parties as well as staff.

  12. Staff has the right to enforce, change and punish as needed in order to maintain the spirit of the server, being worldbuilding and cooperation. (edited)

Effective Casus Bellis

If a Neutral Nation/Town falls into one of these situations, they are at risk of these causes:

War of Reclamation:

-Defected Province- When a province defects due to rebels -Disloyal Vassal- When a vassal breaks free -Reconquest- Reclaiming a town once part of a nation that was taken in a pervious war (Including Rebellion)

War of Indebtment:

-Indebtment- When a indebted nation refuses to follow through on a previous agreement. (If the nation attempts to repay, but is refused, casus belli is null. They No longer have to pay this debt.)

War of Independence:

-Applicable to any town that requests independence (after a majority of active citizens BEFORE the war ticket was created in that town request independence <50%)

These become ineffective/null & void after 2 weeks of occurrence. Ex: Nation A refuses to pay Nation B back after Nation B loaned 20 diamonds with the promise that 64 wool would be given in return. 2 weeks after the initial refusal, Nation B has not declared war. Nation A did not pay the debt and now has no Casus Belli against them

All server rules will continue to evolve over time. This means we may take action against any player or group that violates the spirit of these rules when we encounter a new threat or harm that is not explicitly covered in the current version.
